Introduction

This application may be used to browse CTPS's database of information on various factors associated with the 'livability' of communities. The database includes information on the following factors for the 101 cities and towns in the Boston MPO region

  1. population density
  2. employment density
  3. percentage of the population over age 70
  4. percentage of non-interstate road mileage with sidewalks on at least one side
  5. number of miles of non-interstate roadways with sidewalks on at least one side
  6. year 2000 journey-to-work resident walk share (see below)
  7. number of miles of off-road bicycle trails
  8. number of miles non-interstate roadways with bicycle lanes or shoulders > 4 feet wide
  9. percentage of non-interstate roadways with bicycle lanes or shoulders > 4 feet wide
  10. year 2000 journey-to-work resident bicycle share (see below)
  11. number of automobiles per household
  12. number of daily vehicle miles traveled (VMT) per household
  13. pedestrian crash rate: number of crashes involving pedestrians per year per 1,000 residents
  14. bicycle crash rate: number of crashes involving bicyclists per year per 1,000 residents

Journey to Work Mode Share Data

Journey to Work information was compiled by the United States Census Bureau using data derived from the long form questionnaire administered during the year 2000 decennial Census of Population. For more information, see the Census Bureau's Journey to Work and Place of Work web page.


Using the Application

This application allows the data to be queried and displayed in three ways:

  1. Setting a theme for the map display by selecting a theme (such as Population Density) from the "Select Map Theme" combo box.
  2. Livability data for a particular town or city may be obtained by selecting a municipality in the "Select A Municipality" combo box. This data is displayed in the tabbed panel at the bottom of the application window.
  3. Data for the currently selected map theme for a particular town or city can be obtained by clicking on a municipality in the map display with your mouse. The selected municipality is highlighted on the map, and the data for it is displayed in the tabbed panel at the bottom of the application window.


Data Sources

The data displayed by this application come from:

  • United States Bureau of the Census
  • Massachusetts Office of Geographic Information (MassGIS)
  • Massachusetts Department of Transportation (MassDOT)
  • Boston Region Metropolitan Planning Organization
  • Metropolitan Area Planning Council (MAPC)
